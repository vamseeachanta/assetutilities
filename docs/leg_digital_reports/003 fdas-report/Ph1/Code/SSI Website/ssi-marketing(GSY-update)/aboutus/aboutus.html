Who We Are

Subsea Systems manufactures custom equipment to customer specifications serving the energy sector. Subsea Systems policy is to provide exceptional customer satisfaction through safety, quality, delivery, compliance to customer required specifications, continuous improvement and respect for the environment.

Subsea Systems is certified to API Q1 and ISO 9001:2008 and our team is committed to lean Manufacturing and Continual Improvement. We achieve excellence and profitability through empowered employees, repeatable processes, innovation and technology. Every product we manufacture is very important to us. Our goal is to do it right the first time.

Facilities

Subsea Systems facility spans over 5 acres and is located in the heart of energy fabrication region in North West Houston.

The manufacturing Building spans 42,000 sq.ft and can easily accommodate heavy projects. An office building of 8,500 sq.ft can accommodate up to 20 persons for project support. We also possess a 100 ft long hydrotest pit (100 ft by 15 ft by 8 ft) to accommodate full length (up to 90 ft) riser joints for high pressure (up to 25,000 psi) test and certification.

"ISO 9001:2008 specifies requirements for a quality management system where an organization":
